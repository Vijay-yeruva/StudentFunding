Dear Graduate Students,

At the University at Albany, the opportunities for you to excel and prepare yourself for a bright future are simply extraordinary. One such example that we’re launching and are particularly proud of is the “Minerva Fellowship and Assistantship Student Navigator” – an online platform aimed at connecting students to scholarly funding designed to help position them for lifelong success.

A particularly rewarding pathway for students is the successful pursuit of fellowships, research assistantships, and other extramural funding awards that promise to shape your future direction in countless ways. Such awards not only provide direct financial support, but often open new doors to pursue innovative research, education, training and other scholarly activities. Additionally, students have unique opportunities to learn from faculty mentors and advisors, connect with public and private sector organizations, and at the same time, be recognized for special talents, creativity and demonstrated hard work.

Today, over 1,000 active fellowship opportunities nationwide are awaiting your interest. This web platform is designed to showcase how to be successful in competing for these awards and why they are truly worth your time and effort. Specifically, the platform provides highlights on the different types of awards available, best practices and strategies to develop a winning application, and how to connect immediately with a faculty mentor and research administrator to get yourself started.

I encourage you to take some time to navigate and checkout the “Minerva Fellowship and Assistantship Student Navigator” to learn about how to take advantage of the fellowships and assistantship you are eligible for.

We do hope you find this platform valuable and look forward to your pursuit of these promising funding opportunities in the weeks and months ahead.

Sincerely,
