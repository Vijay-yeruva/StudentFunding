General Steps for Applying for a Fellowship

  • Identify the grant you want to apply to
    • Use a search to identify potential grants you are eligible for
    • Read the Funding Opportunity Announcements (FOAs) carefully to determine what is required
      • Make note of any specific requirements, especially the due date
  • Find your Research Administrator (RA)

    The Research Administrator designated for your department is the professional who will help
    you ensure that you’re in compliance with all the requirements of the sponsor and the university

  • Write your application
    • Create an outline
      • What parts are required in your proposal?
        • Make sure that you have all of the required sections in your Proposal
    • Look for template
      • You can often find templates to help you write each section of your proposal for some of the more popular grants
        • NIH F31 Template
        • NSF GRFP Template
        • NSF GRFP Personal Statement Template
        • Generic templates I
        • Generic templates II
    • Carefully review and edit your proposal
      • Check for spelling and grammatical errors, make sure that your proposal is perfect!
    • Submit
      • Follow the instructions as directed in the FOA to submit your proposal
      • Make sure that you submit before the deadline – it will not be accepted late!
