OTHER OPPORTUNITIES

SPIN: world’s largest web-based database for sponsored funding opportunities

SPIN is the world’s largest web-based database for sponsored funding opportunities from federal, state, foundation and university sources. Basic search features are accessible through the campus computing network without a login. Advanced features are available with a SPIN profile, which allows users to access SPIN from any web browser, save searches, create bookmarks, and more.

Get Start on SPIN:

  • Register Using your UAlbany Email
  • Wait for the Administrator to Approve Your Account
  • Once Your Account is Approved – You're Ready to Go!

NOTE: There’s a saved search under “Saved Search” -> “ Public Searches” called “Student Funding Opportunities” for your convenience.

The SPIN Quick Start Guideis available for help.

For technical support, please email Ujash Modi, Research Technology Coordinator at sdat@albany.edu
